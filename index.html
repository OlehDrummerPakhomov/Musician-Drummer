About Me

Hello! I am a professional percussionist with extensive experience in orchestras and ensembles. My passion lies in delivering captivating performances and collaborating with talented musicians. Oleh Pakhomov Orchestra percussionist, drummer, educator. I have been playing music for more than 20 years. Education: * Ternopil Music College named after S. Krushelnytska. Class of Ihor Marushchak. * National Music Academy named after P. Tchaikovsky, class of docent, Honored Artist of Ukraine Oleksandr Blinov. Master degree . Laureate of Ukrainian and international competitions. Since the age of 16, I have been working as an orchestra artist and performing as a soloist. Worked with all the leading ensembles and orchestras of Ukraine (Academic Symphony Orchestra of the National TV-Radio Company "Ukraine", National Academic Symphonic Band of Ukraine, National Presidential Orchestra of Ukraine (Symphonic Orchestra, Principal Timpanist , head of the percussion group). As a guest musician, collaborated with the National Academic Symphony Orchestra of Ukraine, the Academic Symphony Orchestra of the National Philharmonic of Ukraine, the Pop Symphony Orchestra of Ukraine, the National Chamber Ensemble "Kyiv Soloists", the National Ensemble of Soloists "Kyiv Camerata Orchestra", the Classical Music Ensemble named after B. Lyatoshynskyi Since 2018, an artist of the percussion group of the symphony orchestra of the National Academic Opera and Ballet Theater of Ukraine named after T. Shevchenko. Fruitfully cooperate with the orchestras "Kyiv Virtuosi Orchestra", "Kyiv Classik", "Ukrainian Improvisation Orchestra" Ensemble of contemporary music "UHO" Also with modern Ukrainian composers. With pop and rock artists as a drummer and percussionist. Educator of Percussion Instruments at the Kyiv Academy of Arts. Participant of many national and international festivals
